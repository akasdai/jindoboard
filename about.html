About Show Your Jindo

Show Your Jindo is a global photo community dedicated to Jindo dogs — Korea's beloved national treasure. Our mission is to celebrate and share the beauty of Jindos with the world.

What Is a Jindo?

The Korean Jindo (진돗개) is a natural monument of South Korea, originating from Jindo Island in the southwestern tip of the Korean peninsula. Renowned for their loyalty, intelligence, and independence, Jindos have been faithful companions to Korean families for centuries.

Designated as Korea's Natural Monument No. 53 in 1962, the Jindo is one of only two Korean dog breeds recognized by the Fédération Cynologique Internationale (FCI). Their dignified appearance, strong prey drive, and deeply loyal nature make them unique among dog breeds worldwide.

Our Mission

Show Your Jindo was created to build a global home for Jindo lovers. Whether you own a Jindo, are thinking about adopting one, or simply admire these beautiful dogs, this is your community.

We believe every Jindo deserves to be seen. Our platform makes it easy to share moments from daily walks, training sessions, play dates, and everything in between.

How It Works

  • Browse: Scroll through photos submitted by Jindo owners from around the world.
  • Search: Find photos by title, author, or description using the search bar.
  • Upload: Share your own Jindo photos by clicking the Upload button. Add a title, description, and your name to let the community know who's behind the lens.
  • Like: Show appreciation for your favorite photos by tapping the heart icon.

Community Guidelines

We want Show Your Jindo to remain a warm, respectful, and joyful place. When uploading photos, please follow these simple guidelines:

  • Only upload photos of Jindo dogs or Korean native dog breeds.
  • Ensure you own the rights to any photo you upload.
  • Do not upload photos that are offensive, harmful, or unrelated to dogs.
  • Be kind and respectful in all community interactions.
  • Photos of dogs in distress, abuse, or inhumane conditions are strictly prohibited.

We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines.
